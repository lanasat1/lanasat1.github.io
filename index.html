Featured Snooze: University Auditorium

A quaint place to find yourself in-between classes. The auditorium will have you catching Zzz's in no time with its plush benches, dim lighting, and orchestra music softly playing in the background.

One of the most popular nap spots on campus, you will find yourself amongst a lucid community of sleepers who share your love for an afternoon siesta.

Located in a central spot on campus students can easily find their way there from places including, but not limited to, Library West, Turlington and Marston Library!
