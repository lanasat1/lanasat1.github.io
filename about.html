What's GatorSleeps.com?

GatorSleeps.com is dedicated to finding and providing students and guests at the University of Florida an easy an manageable way to find sleeping spots around campus.

GatorSleeps.com is UF's internet home for nappers. Nappers - those people for whom power naps is not something they do from time to time - it's part of who they are.

Most importantly, GatorSleeps.com is a community of the most amazing group of people united by their love of sleeping. From professors to the student with a killer hangover from the night before, but can still make it to his 8am class, GatorSleeps.com is home to the greatest and most influential sleepers on the planet, well at least the Orange and Blue portion of the planet.

We don’t try and re-invent the pillow at GatorSleeps.com. We scour campus looking for all those great traits in a good nap - setting, noise, comfort, amenities, you name it. There’s a reason students throughout Gainesville come to GatorSleeps.com to find out where there next nap in-between classes is going to be. If the best spot is in the stadium, we send you there. From lecture halls, auditoriums, common areas, Library West, Plaza of America's, the Reitz Union, etc we've been there and promise you'll nap like no ones business.
